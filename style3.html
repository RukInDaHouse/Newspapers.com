The best ankle boots and how to wear them now

Ankle boots are a footwear that works on everyone. These tried-and-true shoes can be worn during warm and cold season months, they look great with casual and formal attire, from jeans to fancy dresses. I personally, use them as a transitional footwear from summer to fall. Ankle boots go with virtually everything in your closet. Today’s fashion offers many styles of this footwear, that’s why it’s important to figure out what kind of styles are popular right now and how to wear them with the rest of your wardrobe.

What ankle boots are in style right now

  • Flat Ankle Boots
    • These shoes are both comfortable and stylish. They can be teamed with midi skirts, long floral dresses, denim cut-offs, all sort of jeans, etc. Sure, they are for self-confident women, who don’t care about their height.
  • Heeled Ankle Boots
    • The heeled boots are back with vengeance! You can find them in all sort of bold colors, clashing prints and embellishments. But I do think you should start from the basic black, white, brown and gray designs.
  • Leather And Suede Ankle Boots
    • These are wear-forever classics! Suede style gives its wearer a luxe and high-gloss feeling and look, while a leather design is a proven choice for women who keep things simple and practical.
  • Printed Ankle Boots
    • The bold style will never go out of style. The printed shoes make the wearer look seductive, unexpected and fresh. Try them on with culottes, cropped hem jeans, and shorts. Speaking of print, then think brocade, florals or chinoiserie. Go big, or go home!
  • Red Ankle Boots
    • Now, red is everywhere right now, that’s why it’s no wonder why we see red booties around. If you are brave enough, then be sure to try a monochrome red look, teaming red jumper with red pants and red ankle boots. But I recommend starting from red shoes styled with black leggings and black bomber jacket.
  • White Ankle Boots
    • White boots are like red ones, they are everywhere right now. I do love to see them styled with checkered suits, floral frocks, and denim skirts.
  • Pink Ankle Boots
    • Be sure to invest in a pair of pink boots. Let it be in fuchsia, pastel or millennial pink, they do look awesome with almost any color bottoms.
  • Sock Ankle Boots
    • Sock boots are something gorgeous! This footwear comes in a knitted, mesh, plain or metallic design. This second-skin sock boot goes with anything!
  • Biker Ankle Boots
    • Combat and biker style ankle boots have a serious appearance. They work equally well with ripped denim and floral dresses. I personally like dressing them up in cute dresses.
  • With block heels
    • This 1990’s trend hasn’t gone anywhere. The block heel is incredibly comfortable and it’s perfect for the fashion-conscious woman who wants to keep looking tall.
  • With laces
    • Yes, the laces didn’t go away. The lace-up ankle boots make you look edgier and special. I personally advise trying these shoes with smart-casual separates, or casual dresses.
  • With buckles
    • Buckles look edgier than laces, that’s why women tend to team such footwear with skinny jeans that are either tucked in booties or simply cuffed.
  • Wooden heel
    • The wooden heel ankle boots have a serious look.
  • Gold heel
    • Oh, yes, gold heel ankle boots. This is a simple twist that makes classic booties look a bit exclusive and wanted.
